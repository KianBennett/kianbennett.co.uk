Ace Defender
View on itch.io
Ace Defender was made over three weeks in groups of six to fit the theme 'Star Fox / R-Type'. The player must control their ship to shoot oncoming enemies with varying movement/shooting patterns, and can of course do a barrel roll to deflect enemy shots. There is an intro cutscene and tutorial level explaining the game's controls led by a charming companion character.

The game was made in Unity with C# - I took the role of programmer and 3d modeller in our group. It ranked second place in our year group.
