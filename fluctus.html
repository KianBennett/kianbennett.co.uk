Fluctus
View on itch.io
Fluctus was made in a week to fit the theme of 'hyper-casual single input game'. The curve of the level is generated from a sine wave with varying amplitude and frequency affecting the difficulty. The player can upload their score to an online leaderboard facilitated by dreamlo.

The game was made in Unity with C#, and it ranked 5th in our year group (out of 65).
